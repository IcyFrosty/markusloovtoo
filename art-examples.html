Card Art Examples

AI-generated concept art for Helix Alpha cards. These are examples of what the final printed cards could look like — style and theme can be adjusted.

Villain

The Stalker

ATK 10  |  DEF 10  |  MOV 4

At the start of the villain turn, the Stalker moves directly towards the closest player. If it's already on the same tile as a player, it attacks them — twice. A relentless hunter that never stops pursuing.

Villain

Necromancer

ATK 3  |  DEF 3  |  MOV 3

Weak alone, but terrifying over time. At the beginning of the villain turn, roll a D6 and add the number of players. If the total is 6 or more, a zombie spawns in a random location. The board fills with undead.

Survivor

Fighter

ATK 4  |  DEF 3  |  MOV 4

The team's combat specialist. Can equip 2 weapons simultaneously, and all equipped weapons grant +1 ATK. Defeating an enemy gives the Fighter an extra action — momentum is everything.

Event

Meteorite

Random Coordinate  |  4 Tile Radius

Choose a random coordinate using D20 dice. Everyone within a 4-tile radius is knocked out. Even the villain gets stunned if caught in the blast. Pure chaos from the sky.

Tool

Unstable Plasma Cannon

Single Use  |  High Risk

Use this item to stun an enemy instantly. But then roll a D6 — if you get a 1 or 2, you get knocked out by the backfire. Discard after use. Desperate times call for desperate weapons.
